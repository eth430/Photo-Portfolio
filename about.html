Ethan Saunders is a photographer based out of Chicago, IL. He first picked up a camera 8 years ago and has since honed his skills in many disiplines of photography including cityscape, landscape, product, and portrait. His portfolio consists of a variety of subjects spanning across the country. Through his work, one can see the passion he has for exploring the world around him and capturing the details using unique techniques, fresh angles, and elevated perspectives. One can expect the highest level of professionalism and quality in working with him.
