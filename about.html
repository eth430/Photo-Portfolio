Ethan Saunders is a photographer based out of Chicago, IL and Minneapolis, MN. He first picked up a camera 6 years ago and currently specializes in cityscape, portrait, and landscape photography. His portfolio consists of a variety of subjects spanning across the country. Through his work, one can see his passion as he strives to keep things fresh and unique with new angles, frames, and perspectives. One can expect the highest level of professionalism and quality in working with him.
